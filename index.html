About me

I want to use my skills in front-end development, process improvement & human behavior to help others. This comes in the form of helping companies live out their mission and helping individuals utilize technology as a tool to improve the things they care about.

Some of my specific passions are edtech, child & family, healthcare tech, & sustainability.

Interested in digging a little deeper?

I have always been passionate about learning & organization – I can still tell you what color-coordinated folders I’d attach to which subject (science = green, math = red). I majored in Psychology in college but found myself drawn to the technical side of the social sciences. While working in a research lab, I both administered assessments and worked with the assessment data, and I soon realized that I much preferred contributing to the lab's mission from behind-the-scenes.

I then worked as an operations specialist, where I validated skills assessments, built trackers, and automated manual processes through formulas and SQL queries. My projects & responsibilities ranged in scope from manual QA to research coordinator to project manager & developer. I loved learning something new with each task, and it was in this role that I discovered how to combine my interest in human behavior while challenging myself in new ways technically. This culminated in leading a large-scale accessibility project that brought an entire assessment library into full WCAG compliance. That experience ignited my interest in pursuing a career in UX development.

To pursue this interest, I have been eagerly honing my skills in front-end languages, frameworks, and design principles. Recently, I obtained a certificate in Web Development from the UC-Berkeley extension program, and I am excited to continue growing as a developer while driving my passion for continuous learning.

I value collaboration and communication. I enjoy serving as the connection point between technically-oriented and content-oriented people on the team. I value both perspectives to ensure that all needs are met and understood. I bring positivity, eagerness to learn new things, and strong attention to detail to every project I work on.

It did not disappoint.
